About the Platform

The Farm Trade Platform System is a web-based platform designed to connect farmers directly with buyers. It helps farmers access real-time market prices, compare prices from different markets, and sell produce at fair prices without relying on middlemen.

Key Features

User Registration

Farmers and buyers can create accounts and login securely.

Produce Listing

Farmers can upload produce details and prices.

Market Prices

Users can compare real-time prices from different markets.

Buyer Connection

Buyers can contact farmers directly without middlemen.

Notifications

Receive updates about market changes and offers.

Reports & Analytics

Track sales, profits, and market trends.
